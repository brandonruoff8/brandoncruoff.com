Pokémon Black and White

Pokémon Black 2 and White 2

Generation 5 is undoubtly the best generation. People mock the designs of the Pokémon from that generation, but no matter what is said, the designs will always be better than generation 1. Generation 5 took energy out of making every single design look super cool and put it into creating the most original Pokémon out of any generation ever. That's right, Generation 5 by itself included more original Pokémon then the "OG" region itself. Not to mention that generation 5 also included continuous animations during battle which hadn't been done before, creation of the Battle Spot for online battles, and of course, the best in-game story of any Pokémon game so far. This is the only generation where the games following the original releases were sequels, rather than a simple remake of the same game with a few decent changes.
