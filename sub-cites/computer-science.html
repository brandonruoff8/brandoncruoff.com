Mini Calculator

I built a simple Calculator program build from JFrame for one of my classes. It can take two numbers as input at a time in the following format:

[integer1][function][integer2]
(for example: 22+8)

It cannot use decimals as an input, but the division function will in fact supply a decimal answer.
